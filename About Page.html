About InvestSmart Academy

InvestSmart Academy is dedicated to empowering Canadians with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the stock market confidently. Founded by experienced investors, our mission is to make stock market education accessible, practical, and focused on Canadian-specific topics like the Toronto Stock Exchange (TSX), tax-advantaged accounts, and regulatory compliance.

Whether you're a beginner looking to understand basics or an advanced trader seeking strategies for volatile markets, our courses are designed to help you invest smartly and build long-term wealth.
